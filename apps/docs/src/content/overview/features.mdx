## Current Features

### Content & Engagement
- **HackerNews aggregation**: Browse and share trending stories with your commentary
- **Rich post creation**: Create posts with images, audio, video, code files, and documents
- **Comments & discussions**: Engage in threaded conversations on posts
- **Voting system**: Upvote/downvote posts to show appreciation and affect Aura
- **Bookmarking**: Save posts and HackerNews stories for later
- **Tags & mentions**: Organize content with tags and mention users in posts

### Social Features
- **User profiles**: Customizable profiles with avatars, bios, and activity feeds
- **Follow system**: Follow users and discover new people through suggestions
- **Notifications**: Real-time alerts for follows, comments, mentions, and votes
- **User discovery**: Browse trending and new users, get personalized suggestions

### Reputation System
- **Aura points**: Earn reputation through quality contributions
- **Transparent logging**: All Aura changes are tracked and auditable
- **Anti-gaming measures**: Caps and validation to prevent system abuse

### Technical Excellence
- **Type‑safe APIs**: End-to-end type safety with TypeScript and Prisma
- **Fast performance**: Next.js 15 with Turbopack, Redis caching, optimized queries
- **A11y‑first**: Components follow accessibility best practices
- **Search**: Fast full-text search powered by Meilisearch

## Roadmap

### Planned Features
- **Communities**: Topic-driven spaces for focused discussions
- **Direct messaging**: Private conversations and group chats
- **Zeph AI**: Context-aware assistant for content discovery and creation
- **Multi-platform aggregation**: Support for Reddit, Twitter, and more platforms
- **Advanced moderation**: Community-driven moderation tools
- **API access**: Public REST and GraphQL APIs for third-party integrations

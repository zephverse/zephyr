## What is Zephyr?

Zephyr is what happens when someone gets tired of living in 12 browser tabs and decides to do something about it.

It is a social platform, but not in the "here's another place to doom scroll" way. Zephyr is built around the idea that your online life is fragmented across too many places, and that a single intelligent platform should hold all of it together without making you feel like you are using a Swiss Army knife that is also on fire.

You post. You discover. You engage. You build a reputation. And the longer you are here, the more Zephyr understands you — what you care about, what you create, what kind of chaos you enjoy at 2am. It is a living, breathing feed that grows with you, not against you.

Oh, and it is open source. Because the audacity of building this should be public.

<Callout type="info">
  Zephyr lives inside a wider ecosystem called the <strong>Zephverse</strong> a warm, cozy, slightly chaotic corner of the internet where your feed, your Aura, your communities, and Zeph all coexist peacefully. Think of Zephyr as the platform and the Zephverse as the feeling.
</Callout>

## What is actually inside

Zephyr is not a single thing. It is every thing, done right, in one place.

A feed that aggregates content from across the internet alongside your own posts, because why should you choose between what you create and what you consume. Communities where people gather around ideas, not just accounts. Threaded conversations that go as deep as the topic deserves. A media layer that lets images, videos and files breathe the way they were meant to. Real-time chat for when the conversation absolutely cannot wait. Tipping and exclusive content for when your work deserves more than just a like.

And running underneath all of it a single reputation layer, a single algorithm, a single AI companion that remembers everything.

Most platforms ask you to pick one thing and live there. Zephyr just says yes to all of it.

## The Aura System

<Callout type="info">
  Aura is transparent and auditable. Every change is logged with its type, amount and source so nothing is ever a mystery.
</Callout>

Most platforms have likes. Zephyr has **Aura**.

Aura is your unified reputation score across everything you do on the platform. Post something brilliant? Aura goes up. Get tipped on a piece of content? Aura goes up. Contribute meaningfully to a community? You guessed it. Aura is not just a vanity number — it is a living record of your presence, your contributions, and your credibility across the entire Zephverse.

Think of it like XP in a game, except the game is the internet and the leaderboard is your entire social identity.

Aura is capped at 150 per post so nobody games the system by posting "hello" forty times a day. The algorithm will not let you. Zeph definitely judged you for trying.

Aura improves your content discoverability and highlights quality contributors without gating any core features. You are not locked out of anything for having low Aura. You are just, you know, less legendary.

## The Algorithm

Here is the thing about most recommendation algorithms: they optimize for engagement, which is a polite word for "we figured out what makes you anxious and we are leaning into it."

Zephyr's algorithm is different not because we are morally superior (we are not, we are just developers), but because it has more to work with. Every interaction you have across every feature posts, communities, bookmarks, content you engage with, content you skip feeds a single unified recommendation layer.

It is not "here are things similar to the last thing you clicked." It is "here is a picture of what you actually care about, assembled from everything you have done here." The algorithm learns across contexts, not just within one. Which means the more you use Zephyr, the more eerily accurate it gets.

You have been warned.

## Zeph

<Callout type="warning">
  Zeph is under active development. It is going to get smarter, more capable, and slightly more opinionated over time. Updates ship fast.
</Callout>

Every platform has an AI assistant now. Most of them have the memory of a goldfish with a concussion.

**Zeph** is Zephyr's AI companion and the key difference is context. Zeph is woven into the entire platform not bolted on as an afterthought. It remembers what you have posted, what you have bookmarked, what communities you are in, what you have been working on. It retains memory across every corner of the Zephverse.

Here is what Zeph actually does:

**It understands context.** Ask Zeph to summarize a long community thread and it already knows which parts you have read. Ask it to help you write a post and it already knows your voice. No re-explaining yourself every single time.

**It helps you create.** Draft posts from rough notes, refine tone and clarity, generate titles and outlines, suggest replies all editable, all aligned to how you actually write, never auto-posted without your say.

**It respects your control.** Opt-in context usage, clear controls, citations when content comes from external sources, and per-conversation memory you can reset whenever you want. Zeph is a companion, not a surveillance tool.

It is not a chatbot. It is a companion that has been paying attention.

## The Big Idea

The internet has a fragmentation problem. Great content, great people and great conversations are scattered across dozens of platforms that do not talk to each other and do not care about you as a whole person. You are a user ID on each of them. A data point. A retention metric.

Zephyr is built on the premise that one platform, done right, with a smart enough layer underneath, can make all of that irrelevant. Not by replacing everything but by becoming the place where everything makes sense together.

Aura gives you identity. The algorithm gives you signal. Zeph gives you memory. The platform gives you everything else.

This is not a feature list. It is a different way of thinking about what social software should actually do for the people who use it.
